Welcome to my website! My name is Chris and in this space I aim to detail a portfolio of projects, achievements, and professional goals. As of December 2023, I have just graduated with a PhD from Princeton University's Department of Chemical & Biological Engineering, where I researched the mechanical relationships between complex flows and deformable structures in the lab of Pierre-Thomas Brun. In searching for a full-time job, I would like to unite my technical skillset and interest in solving complex problems with a passion for communication, design, and accessibility.
