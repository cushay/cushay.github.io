Welcome! My name is Chris and in this space I aim to detail a portfolio of professional experience, interests, achievements, and goals. As of early 2024, I have graduated with a PhD from Princeton University's Department of Chemical & Biological Engineering, where I researched the mechanical relationships between complex flows and deformable structures in the lab of Pierre-Thomas Brun. In my work I aim to unite my technical skillset and interest in solving complex problems with a passion for communication, community-building, and thoughtful design to build a more sustainable and connected future.
